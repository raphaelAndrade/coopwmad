Getting Started:
Finding a job


Improve your English?
Expand your network and meet new people?
Gain Canadian experience and stay in Canada?
Gain an international qualification?
Start a new career path?
All of the above?

Choose which is most important to you.
If your goal is to gain industry experience, then focus your search on the company or industry rather than the job title.
If your goal is to get a specific job title, maybe the company or industry is not very important.

For example, maybe you want to gain sales and marketing experience, but what you sell is not important because you are
gaining skills and experience in the field!

Or maybe you want to be in the hotel industry, so it doesn’t matter what position you work in because you are gaining
industry insight!

With a world that has changed so much, think about the job market and high-demand opportunities.
Think about how your skills and experience fit in.

High demand industries:
E-commerce and online sales
Customer Service
Food and beverage
Distribution and warehouse
Accounting
Healthcare and residential care
Cleaning and sanitization services
Security
Web development
Digital Marketing

There are many different roles and responsibilities within these industries.
