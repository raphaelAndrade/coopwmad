Career Prep for WMAD Students

In the WMAD program you will learn various topics that will provide you with the fundamental knowledge needed to excel in your field. Instructors will teach relevant, industry-related topics such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, React.js, Node.js etc. The best part is that students will have the opportunity to apply skills learned in the classroom in volunteer and freelance projects once they reach proficiency in basic front end coding skills.
